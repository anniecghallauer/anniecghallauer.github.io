Here are a few fun facts about me!



Dancing is my favorite all-time, thing to do! Dance has always been a part of every facet of my life since I was four years old. Today, I work part-time at an "18+" dance studio where we host an "adult dance recital" each year on our business' birthday.
My husband and I are proud, pet parents to our three fur-babies: Bingo (dog), Asher (male cat) and Olive (female cat). We had these cake-topper figurines specially made for our wedding so that our loving animals could be a part of our special day!

We love to spend our weekends as tourists in our own city. Kansas City is a great place to live, and it has grown like crazy in the last decade. Sight-seeing, festivals, coffeeshops, restaurants and bars - our mission is to experience them all!

The most celebrated holiday in our home is Halloween! Each year, my husband & I have thrown a Day of the Dead themed party for our friends and family. We love to go all out by getting dressed up, decorating the house, and making lots of food & drinks!
